# Deploying Predictions

A model only creates value when its predictions reach the people making decisions. This final lesson covers the ways to surface and act on Einstein Discovery output.

## Predictions on a record page

The most common deployment puts the prediction right on the record:

- Add the **Einstein Predictions** Lightning component to a record page.
- It shows the predicted outcome, the top factors, and the prescriptive **improvements**.
- Users see *why* and *what to do* — not just a score.

::note
Because predictions are mapped to a Salesforce object, they update as the record's fields change. A deal that improves its inputs gets a better predicted outcome in near real time.
::

## Writing predictions to a field

To use predictions in reports, automation, or list views, write them to a field:

- Map the model output to a **custom field** on the object.
- Salesforce scores records and stores the value.
- Now the prediction is filterable, sortable, and reportable like any field.

## Predictions in Flow

Einstein Discovery exposes a **prediction action** in Flow Builder:

1. Add the **Get Prediction** element.
2. Pass in the record's fields.
3. Use the returned score and improvements in downstream logic.

This lets you, for example, auto-route high-risk cases or alert reps on deals likely to slip.

## Surfacing predictions in dashboards

Because scored values can live in a field or dataset, you can:

- Build a **risk dashboard** ranking accounts by churn probability.
- Facet predictions by region, segment, or owner.
- Combine descriptive trends with predictive scores on one page.

::tip
Pair a predictive KPI with the prescriptive improvements behind it. "32% churn risk — top lever: increase support touchpoints" is far more actionable than a number alone.
::

## Acting on recommendations

The prescriptive **improvements** are the payoff. Operationalize them by:

- Embedding **actions** on the dashboard or record to apply the suggested change.
- Training users to read the top factors, not just the score.
- Tracking whether acting on recommendations actually moves the metric.

## Closing the loop

The full pipeline now connects end to end:

1. Data flows in and is shaped into datasets.
2. SAQL and dashboards make it explorable.
3. Einstein Discovery predicts outcomes and prescribes actions.
4. Predictions land where users work and drive decisions.
