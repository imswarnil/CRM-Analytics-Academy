# Foundations

Welcome to CRM Analytics Academy. This first module gives you the runway every other module assumes — what CRM and analytics actually are, what CRM Analytics adds on top, and how to get set up so you can follow along hands-on.

## What this module covers

We move in chronological order, from the broadest idea to your first click in the product:

::card-group
  ::card{title="CRM Basics" icon="i-lucide-history" to="/foundations/crm-basics"}
  A short history of CRM and the core concepts — contacts, accounts, the pipeline — that everything else builds on.
  ::

  ::card{title="What Is CRM Analytics?" icon="i-lucide-compass" to="/foundations/what-is-crm-analytics"}
  Where CRM Analytics fits in the Salesforce platform and the five building blocks you will use throughout the course.
  ::

  ::card{title="Setup & Access" icon="i-lucide-key-round" to="/foundations/setup-and-access"}
  Licensing, enabling the platform, and the permission sets your users need to build and view analytics.
  ::

  ::card{title="Touring Analytics Studio" icon="i-lucide-compass" to="/foundations/analytics-studio"}
  A guided tour of the workspace so you know where datasets, recipes, and dashboards live.
  ::
::

## How to use this course

- **Follow the order.** Each module builds on the last; Foundations unlocks everything else.
- **Try it live.** Spin up a free [Salesforce Developer Edition](https://developer.salesforce.com/signup) and follow along.
- **Ask the AI.** Every page is published as Markdown and over MCP, so an assistant can tutor you from the source.

::tip
New to Salesforce entirely? Begin with **CRM Basics** next — it assumes zero prior knowledge and sets up every term we use later.
::
