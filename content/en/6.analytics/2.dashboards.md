# Dashboards & Einstein

A dashboard combines multiple queries and widgets into one interactive page.

## Build a dashboard

Explore a dataset with a **lens**, then clip it onto a dashboard. Add charts, number KPIs, and filter widgets. **Bindings** and **faceting** make selections in one chart update the others.

## Make it interactive

Add drill-downs, conditional formatting against targets, and Salesforce **actions** so users can act on insights without leaving the dashboard.

## Add predictions with Einstein Discovery

**Einstein Discovery** analyzes a dataset to explain an outcome and predict it. Create a **story**, review the top factors, deploy a **model**, and surface predictions on records and in flows.
