## Signup gotchas to avoid

Salesforce signups are easy, but a few small things trip people up. Save yourself the frustration:

- **Use a valid email.** You will need to click a verification link, so make sure you can access the inbox.
- **Any username works.** The username is in email format but does not have to match your real email — it just has to be unique across Salesforce. Pick something you will remember.
- **Country sets the language.** The country you choose controls the default language of your org. If you want everything in English, **pick United States**.
- **Open the verification link in an incognito/private window.** If you are already logged into another Salesforce org, clicking the link in your normal browser can clash with that session. Right-click the **Verify** link and open it in a private/incognito window to keep things clean.
- **Watch out for corporate email.** Some company email systems wrap links inside security scanners that can corrupt the verification URL. If your link will not work, simply **sign up again using a different (personal) email address**.

Once you are verified, set your password and you will land inside a brand-new Salesforce org with CRM Analytics ready to go.
