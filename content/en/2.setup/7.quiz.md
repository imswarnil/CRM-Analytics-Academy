# Setup & Provisioning: Graded Quiz

This is the graded exam for the **Setup** section. It draws on everything from provisioning users through building a scoped permission set — licenses versus permission sets, the Integration and Security Users, row-level security, and the org-level Analytics settings. Pass it and the section is officially complete.

::tip{icon="i-lucide-target"}
You need **85% to pass**. Each attempt serves a rotating set of questions drawn from a larger pool, so a failed retry gives you a fresh mix — there is nothing to memorize by position. Your average score across every section is tracked on your profile.
::

Answer every question, then submit to see your result. If you don't pass, review the [interview questions](/setup/interview-questions) or revisit any lesson, then try again with a new set.
