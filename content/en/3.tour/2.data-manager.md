# Data Manager

Data Manager is where data enters CRM Analytics and gets transformed before it reaches a dashboard.

## Connections

A **connection** is a source: the local Salesforce org, another Salesforce org, or an external source via Salesforce Connect. Every recipe and dataflow reads from a connection.

## Dataflows & recipes

- **Recipes** are the visual, drag-and-drop way to transform data — filter, join, bucket, aggregate — and are the recommended starting point.
- **Dataflows** are the older, JSON-based transformation pipelines; you'll still see them in established orgs.

## Monitor

The **Monitor** tab shows every job that has run — dataflows, recipes, syncs — with status, duration, and row counts. It's the first place to check when a dataset looks stale or a job fails.
