# Filtering, Faceting & Drilling Down

## Faceting

Clicking a value in one chart — say, a region on a bar chart — **facets** every other connected widget on the page to that selection. Click again to clear it. This is the default interaction in CRM Analytics and needs no configuration.

## Explicit filters

Filter widgets (dropdowns, date pickers, toggles) let a viewer set a condition without needing a chart to click on — useful for fields that aren't already charted, like "Fiscal Quarter."

## Drilling down

Some charts support **drill paths** — clicking a bar in "Region" reveals the next level, like "Country," without leaving the chart. Drill paths are configured by the dashboard builder ahead of time.

## Clearing selections

A **Clear** or **Reset filters** action (often in the top toolbar) removes all facets and filters at once, returning the dashboard to its default view.
