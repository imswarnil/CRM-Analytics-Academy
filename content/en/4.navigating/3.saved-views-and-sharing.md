# Saved Views & Sharing

## Saved views

After applying filters you use often, save them as a **saved view** — your personal default when you reopen the dashboard, without changing it for anyone else.

## Sharing a dashboard

Dashboards inherit the sharing of the **app** they live in. To give someone access, share the app (or a specific asset within it) as Viewer, Editor, or Manager rather than sharing a raw URL.

## Exporting

Most charts support **Export** to CSV or the underlying query results, useful for one-off analysis outside CRM Analytics. Full-dashboard PDF snapshots are also available from the toolbar.
