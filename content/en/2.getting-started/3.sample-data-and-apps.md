# Explore Sample Apps & Data

You don't need production data to learn CRM Analytics. Salesforce ships sample apps you can install straight from Analytics Studio.

## Install a sample app

From the **Create** menu, choose **App**, then pick one of the featured **Sample Apps** (such as Sales Analytics or Service Analytics). Salesforce provisions the dataflow, datasets, and dashboards automatically.

## Practice on your own org data

If your org already has Opportunities, Accounts, or Cases, you can build a quick dataset from them without a full recipe — useful for testing chart types and bindings before investing in a real pipeline.

## Clean up when you're done

Sample apps count against your org's row and app limits. Delete ones you no longer need from **Analytics Studio → Apps**.
